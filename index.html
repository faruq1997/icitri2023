About

The 2nd International Conference on Information Technology Research and Innovation (ICITRI) is organized by Universitas Nusa Mandiri and sponsored by IEEE Indonesia Section. This conference series serves as a forum for sharing knowledge and research in computer and information science and provides a platform for researchers and practitioners from both academia as well as industry to meet and share cutting-edge development.

AUTHOR GUIDELINE

Please prepare your manuscripts according to the IEEE Conference Paper format. The manuscript should be 4 to 6 pages long. Research paper should explore a specific technology problem and propose a complete solution to it, with experimental results.
